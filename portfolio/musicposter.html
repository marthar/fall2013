Music Poster

I designed this poster for one of my favorite songs, Only Time, by Enya. This beautiful song reminds us that you don't know where your life will lead you, so relax, go with the flow, and time will unveil your path. The poster expresses the serene feeling you get from listening to this song. My poster was inspired by the designer David Lance Goines.
