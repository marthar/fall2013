Wedding Cake Box Display

I created this large Wedding Cake box to accompany pre-existing cake flatware packaging. The flatware boxes, shaped as cake slices, each held a cake knife and server. My wedding cake box has been used when displaying the items at a show. It gives context to the individual cake knife and server boxes when on display.

Dimensions: 1' 4" tall and 2' 4" wide.
