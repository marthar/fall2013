I Like Words

I Like Words and Pronunciation combines Robert Piroshs letter, I Like Words, and the popular poem, The Chaos, by Gerard Nolst Trenite. The two texts come together to help one better understand the complex and confusing English Language, showing that when words are properly understood, they can be fun to use, and make a bold statement. Robert Piroshs, I Like Words, aids this idea; he submitted this letter as a job application, which landed his dream job with MGM as a screenwriter.

I created handmade textures for each page to help enhance the meaning, as well as printed, and produced the physical copy of the book myself.
