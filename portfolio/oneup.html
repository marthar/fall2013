The One Up Experience

We all have goals, and the intention of having these goals is to actively make our life better. By working on the things that we want to accomplish, we are putting ourselves first, and doing something that will make us happier later. Although this seems like a great plan, it's not always that simple to fufill your goals. Along the way there are things that distract us, and it's easy to give up when you don't see an instant reward for staying true to your goal.

The One Up Experience adds gamification to your everyday life, and seamlessly keeps track of your goals using Augmented Reality Contact Lenses and the One Up app. After choosing and setting up a goal, simply send the challenge to a friend and wait for an approval. The challenge can be bet based (based on a bet sum of money), object based (based on an object you agree to compete for), or action based (based on an action such as, "the loser will cook the winner dinner for the rest of the week"). The ability to place bets and compete for money or points allows you to be rewarded each time you complete a step towards your goal, and have a reason to continue working towards the end result. After your goals have been set up, there is no need to put information into the app. The Augmented Reality Contact lenses use image recognition to keep up with your progress. One Up makes your goals your reality.
