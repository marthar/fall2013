Eat Here

With so many options, its not always easy to choose a restaurant, especially when you are out with a group of people who all have different ideas of where they want to eat. Eat Here is a community based app that provides you with insider tips from locals to help you decide where to eat. A quick questionnaire will help you weed through restaurants to find the perfect place for you and your group.

Eat Here

Helps you decide where to eat. Provides you with insider tips. Ensures you have a great meal.

Around Me

Restaurants around you can be filtered by distance, price, food type, and atmosphere. Map view allows you to see exactly where the restaurants are, as well as insider tips. List view displays a bit more information, including the restaurant's hours and insider tips.

Questionnaire

By filling out the brief questionnaire you can quickly solve your dilemma of where to eat. Questions refering to group size, atmosphere, food type, food alergies, and time of day help you find the perfect restaurant to suit all of your needs.

Restaurant Page

In a specific restaurant's view, you can see information, insider tips, photography, and the menu. If you decide this restaurant isn't for you, its easy to click on another restaurant that is close by using the map above.
