Wash and pick over the lentils.

Place lentils in a saucepan and cover with water.

Bring to a boil.

Cook, covered, about 20 minutes.

Meanwhile, in a large casserole, brown the onions in oil.

Stir in the garlic and coriander.

Add the spinach and sauté 5 to 6 minutes, stir them frequently.

Add the potatoes, lentils, and enough lentil cooking liquid to cover everything in the pot.

Season with salt and pepper.

Bring to a boil, lower the heat, and simmer for 1 hour or until it gets thick and soupy.

Stir in the lemon juice.

Serve hot, lukewarm or cold.
