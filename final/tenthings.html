Ten Things

The Ten Things Every Celiac Should Know is an informative book on Celiac Disease. I wrote it with a few family members in mind who I know could benefit from learning more about their newfound illness. As the disease becomes more popular, I wanted the information to be readily available, in a well presented way that is interesting for the reader.
